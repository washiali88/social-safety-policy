Social App Safety Standards

Protecting Our Community with Highest Safety Standards

Zero Tolerance Policy

Social App strictly prohibits any child sexual abuse material (CSAM), exploitation, or harmful content. We maintain a zero-tolerance approach to ensure a safe environment for all users.

Safety Measures Implemented

• Automated content moderation systems

• Manual review processes for reported content

• User reporting system with priority response

• Age verification and restriction protocols

• Regular staff training on safety protocols

Legal Compliance

Our app complies with all regional and international laws regarding child protection, including reporting obligations to appropriate authorities. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies.
